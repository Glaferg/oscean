Point on a circle

To convert a degree angle to a radian.

radian = deg * (PI / 180)

Find angle between two points

angle = atan2(b.y - a.y, b.x - a.x) + radian

Find distance between two points

sqrt((a.x - b.x) * (a.x - b.x) + (a.y - b.y) * (a.y - b.y))

Find point on a circle

x = a.x + r * cos(angle)
y = a.y + r * sin(angle)
